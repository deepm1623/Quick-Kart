Frequently asked

Everything you need to know about subscriptions.
Can I skip a delivery?
Absolutely. Skip any delivery up to 48 hours before your scheduled slot. No penalties, no questions.
What if I want to cancel?
Cancel anytime from your account dashboard. No cancellation fees, and you keep all benefits until your current cycle ends.
Can I customize my box?
Yes! Set dietary preferences, allergies, and favorite categories. Our algorithm learns your taste and curates accordingly.
How do I change my delivery address?
Update your address anytime in account settings. Changes apply to your next scheduled delivery.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and digital wallets. Auto-billing happens on your renewal date.
Do you deliver to my area?
We currently serve major metros and tier-1 cities. Enter your pin code during signup to check availability.
